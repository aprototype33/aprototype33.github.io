Esha Villa Drupadi II is designed in a fusion of elegant classy style. The villa offers a large space with taste of luxury during your stay in Bali. The interior is tastefully decorated in white dominance with natural balance nuance.


Still the villa offer a relaxing space a pool surrounded by lush tropical garden. Indulge your self with a total relaxation in this piece of little heaven just few steps away from the Center of Seminyak.

The architectural style at Esha Villa Drupadi II is a fusion of classy elegant style for a spacious villa. The interior is tastefully decorated with dominant white with silver and black accent. Still high quality wood material is added to the component to bring balance with its natural nuance. Both Esha Villa Drupadi I & II are nestled side by side on the well known Jalan Drupadi in the heart of Seminyak, within easy walking to restaurants and boutiques in both Jalan Legian and Laksmana (Oberoi).
